
# Mockingbird Electric WaaS Strategy

## A) PROJECT SUMMARY
The goal is to generate inbound phone calls for Mockingbird Electric, a local Pasadena electrician, using a fast, trust-driven, single-page website. The site is built for high conversion, prioritizing mobile users who need immediate electrical help.

## B) SITE STRUCTURE & SECTION ORDER
1. **Hero Section**: Urgent headline, primary offer (Free Inspection), and immediate "Call Now" CTA.
2. **Trust Signals Bar**: 5-Star reviews, License status, and Local status indicators.
3. **Core Services**: Grid of 6 top electrical services with clear icons.
4. **Why Choose Us**: Value propositions (Local, Response Time, Safety).
5. **Service Area Coverage**: List of cities centered around Pasadena, TX.
6. **Reviews / Social Proof**: Local customer snippets.
7. **Process**: Simple 1-2-3 path to getting power restored.
8. **FAQ Section**: Standard electrician queries for SEO and trust.
9. **Final CTA / Footer**: Persistent contact info and location details.
